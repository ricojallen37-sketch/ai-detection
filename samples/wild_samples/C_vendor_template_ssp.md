# Wild Sample C — Vendor Template SSP Excerpt

**Source description:** Representative of the boilerplate-style SSP narratives that appear in many vendor compliance templates and managed-service provider deliverables. The same paragraph structure repeats across multiple controls with only the control reference changed. This is the "cookie-cutter" pattern CMMC assessors flag as a credibility downgrade under NIST 800-171A objective 3.12.4[a]-[d] (the SSP must be developed for THIS system, not a template).

**Control: 3.1.1 Limit System Access to Authorized Users**

Our organization has implemented appropriate technical and procedural controls to satisfy this requirement. Policies and procedures have been established, documented, and communicated to all relevant personnel. Implementation is verified through periodic review by the designated control owner. Evidence of implementation is maintained and available for inspection upon request.

This control is implemented in accordance with industry best practices and applicable regulatory requirements. The control owner is responsible for ongoing maintenance and continuous improvement. Deviations are tracked through our exception management process and remediated in accordance with our risk management framework.
