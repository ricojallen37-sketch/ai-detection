## The Three Tiers

| Tier | Name | Price | What's included |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **AI Evidence Integrity Diagnostic** (standalone) | **$5,500 one-time** | Run the engine on the contractor's submitted SSP, POA&M, and audit log evidence. Deliver the 3-page Integrity Report (verdict, factual matches, risk delta, rebuttal kit). One follow-up call to walk through findings. |

## How the price was set

Round 1 war-panel pressure test (April 22, 2026) asked all four panels to recommend a standalone overlay price for the AI Evidence Integrity Report. Results:

| Panel | Price recommendation | Reasoning summary |
|---|---|---|
| ChatGPT | $8,500 | "Materially serious without crossing into mini-assessment engagement pricing territory; above $10K small DIBs compare you against a real consultant-led mock audit." |
| Grok | $6,500 | "Small DIB contractors already staring at $75K-$150K total Level 2 spend treat $7.5K+ as another line-item that needs ironclad ROI proof, while $6,500 feels like a high-value, low-friction foot-in-door audit." |
| Perplexity | $5,000 | "Consultant-grade money at $7.5K-$10K, but as a net-new wedge for 100-300 employee DIBs already staring at $30K-$100K+ Level 2 costs, $5K is the cleanest impulse-yes price." |
| Gemini | $4,500 | "Price it as a 'Risk Diagnostic' to get them in the door; use the 1.00 synthetic score as the 'Check Engine' light that converts them into the full $25K Hardseal remediation." |
| **Average** | **$6,125** | |
| **Midpoint of range** | **$5,500** | |

**Decision:** $5,500. Sits at the midpoint of the panel range, below the original $7,500-$10,000 hypothesis, and matches Gemini's "Check Engine light, not the repair bill" positioning that converts prospects into the full $25K Sprint.

**Conversion line:** "Score is bad? You don't need another diagnostic. You need the $25K Readiness Sprint, where this scan is bundled and we fix what it found."

## What changes from v0.2

The v0.2 hypothesis was $7,500-$10,000. v0.3 reprices to $5,500 because:

1. **Three of four panels said price below $7,500.** Only ChatGPT held the upper band.
2. **Buyer math:** small DIBs are already burning $30K-$150K on the full Level 2 path. A $5,500 line item clears the impulse-yes threshold; a $10,000 line item triggers a "do I really need this?" review.
3. **The wedge is upsell, not standalone.** $5,500 buys the door. The follow-up conversion is $25K. Don't price the wedge so high it competes with the destination product.

## Pricing review trigger

Re-evaluate if any of these fire:
- 5+ paying customers at $5,500 with zero pushback (signal: priced too low, raise to $6,500).
- 5+ qualified prospects walk after seeing the price (signal: priced too high or wrong positioning, drop to $4,500 or rebundle).
- A direct competitor enters the AI-evidence detection space at any price point (signal: re-baseline against their pricing).
- The Readiness Sprint price changes (signal: the wedge ratio needs to stay roughly 1:5 between standalone and bundle).
