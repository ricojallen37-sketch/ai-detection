# SSP §3.1.1 — Access Control (AC.L2-3.1.1)

## Implementation narrative

Hardseal limits system access to authorized users, processes acting on behalf
of authorized users, and devices in accordance with Policy-AC-001 v2.0 (Access
Control Policy). Administrative accounts are enforced through the Okta tenant
`hardseal.okta.com` with YubiKey 5 hardware tokens per FIDO2/WebAuthn.

Remote access for in-scope engineers follows the terms set out in
Policy-AC-002 v1.0 (Remote Access Standard), which restricts access to the
corporate VPN at `vpn.hardseal.example` and disables split-tunneling.

Account onboarding is performed by Help Desk against the workflow documented
in Policy-IA-005 v1.3 (Identification and Authentication Policy). Offboarding
is tracked in ServiceNow under the ticket family `TKT-OFF-*` with a 24-hour
SLA from HR notification.

Encryption of CUI in transit is governed by Media Protection Policy v3.1,
which requires TLS 1.3 for all northbound data flows.
